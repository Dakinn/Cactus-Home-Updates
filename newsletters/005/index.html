A foundation of Mental Wellness /   Ad Age, Norm and Airport Security /
When Cactus set out to develop the 2013 integrated brand awareness campaign for the Mental Health Center of Denver, our goal was to promote brand recognition, increase understanding of the depth, breadth and value of their services, and stimulate mental health literacy in the Denver-metro community. In July, Cactus CCO Norm Shearer headed off to Advertising Age's 2013 Small Agency Conference and Awards. He met some cool people, picked up an award for Cactus and had a slight snafu with airport security. Here is his action-packed report.
